Data: Citibike, September 2016, 00:00:00 - 05:00:00



There were 34,176 late night trips in September, with an average of 1,139 trips per night. The average trip lasted 16 minutes. Typical late night citibiker was a male who born between 1981 and 1992, subscribing annual membership.

Most of the trips made from and to East Village, Lower East Side, and West Village and the most popular stations were Cooper Square & E 7 St, Allen St & Stanton St and W 4 St & 7 Ave S. Maps are showing trips from those stations.
